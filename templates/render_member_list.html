Member list

This page is showing a member_list object in the voting system. Member lists contain of members (which are pairs of a member's nickname and their respective Bitcoin voting address). In case a member list changes through voting action (new members voted in or old ones voted out), a member list will also refer to a previous member list. Additionally, a member list contains information on the members in official positions (secretary, president, ...). Members are current if they have voted on an issue within the last year. This is noted in the "eligible" column below. Members listed here which are not eligible are not members anymore as per the BU articles.

One member list is marked as current in the system, and it is that member list which is checked to see whether a member is eligible to vote on proposals or in member elections.
